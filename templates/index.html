Heart Disease Classifier

Cardiovascular diseases (CVDs) can be fatal, taking an estimated 17.9 million lives each year, which accounts for 31% of all deaths worldwide. Four out of 5 CVD deaths are due to heart attacks and strokes, and one-third of these deaths occur prematurely in people under 70 years of age. Herein, we are using 11 input variables to predict the likelihood of heart disease.
